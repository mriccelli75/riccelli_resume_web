User Experience Case Study

ONCC.org rebrand and website redesign. Here I was the UX/UI lead. Worked with project managers, internal stakeholders, business analysts and outside marketing vendor to iterate over data derived from brainstorming sessions. In the end it was decided on developing a new icon that represented all of the cancer nursing banners. It was group effort to generate the new information architecture and sitemap. From there I did the wireframes and worked with drupal developers to implement the newly branded UI components.
