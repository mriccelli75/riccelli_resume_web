To continue to grow professionally. I spend every day learning new things because I am endlessly curious. In the world of design, there's always room for growth and continued education.

We work in an agile workflow process. We put focus on requirements that come from either user interviews, or internal people that know what our nurses/users want. The requirements become user stories. We have created user personas on some projects. For most projects, we create the user stories and acceptance criteria based on the requirements. This allows for the designer and developers to work from the users point of view, and not just what a designer or developer would want. I use Adobe XD for prototyping and design comps, and then do most of the HTML/CSS and Javascript front-end work in the browser.

Outside of work, I am a dad of two young girls. I spend most of my time attending their evening activities. When I am not doing that I am enjoying the outdoors. There's nothing a good hike in the woods can't cure. And finally, I can usually be found doing tutorials on YouTube or Udemy early in the morning.
